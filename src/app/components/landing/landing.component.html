Unlock your yield's potential

With Timeless, you can do so much more than putting your money in a yield farm.

Boost your yield, hedge against falling yield rates, or speculate on yield rate changes.

Boost your yield

Do you want to earn more yield? Timeless lets you do just that. Boost your yield by up to 2x for any supported farm.

Hedge against falling yield rates

Worried that yield rates might fall? Timeless enables you to hedge against such risks. Earn a stable yield rate regardless of future rate changes.

Trade future yield changes

Do you think you know how yield rates might change? Timeless lets you put your money where your mouth is. Speculate on future yield rate changes by trading Timeless yield tokens.

Yield tokens do the hard work

Timeless is powered by yield tokens, specially designed ERC-20 tokens whose values are related to yield rates. Each farm corresponds to a set of three yield tokens.

Perpetual Yield Token

Boost and trade yield.

PYT lets its holder claim the yield generated by the underlying assets. PYT goes up in price when the yield rate goes up.

Negative Yield Token

Hedge and trade yield.

The price of NYT moves opposite to how the yield rate moves. If the rate goes up, NYT goes down; if the rate goes down, NYT goes up.

Compounded Perpetual Yield Token

Auto-compound the yield earned into more PYT.

xPYT makes it easier to boost your yield and decreases the cost. It also makes it easier to integrate Timeless into existing DeFi infrastructure.
